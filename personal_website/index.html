PhD Research

Genome sequencing is now inexpensive and fast, we can easily establish the parts list for our cellular components. However, our ability to measure the interactions between those parts hasn't scaled at the same rate; traditional methods allow us to make about 10 interaction measurements per day. With our hacked genome sequencer and custom software and data analysis, we can make more than 10 million measurements of those interactions in under 24 hours.
